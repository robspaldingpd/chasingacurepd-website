Sections that compose.

Pages aren't designed from scratch — they're assembled from these patterns. Each one names what it's for, what it's made of, and where it goes. Mix in any order; the system is the rhythm between them.

06

Story section

Use on: About, StoryPairs with: Quote, photo hero

Two columns: a sticky label on the left, body copy on the right. Drop cap in italic serif orange opens the body. For longform — the founder's story, the science explainer.

The story

It started at the lake.

A backyard concert in 2018. Twenty friends. One reason.

Wendy lost her dad, Roger, to Parkinson's in 2014. Three years later she and Rob threw a concert at their Geist Reservoir home — twenty friends, a borrowed sound system, and a jar for donations. They raised $4,200 and sent every dollar to The Michael J. Fox Foundation.

They did it again the next year. And the next. By 2024, Concert for a Cure had grown to three hundred people and a six-figure check, but the rules hadn't changed: no salaries, every dollar to the science. The lake is still where it happens. The reason is still the same.
